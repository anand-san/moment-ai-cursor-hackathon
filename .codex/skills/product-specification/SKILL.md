# Product Overview - MomentAI

MomentAI is a calm, judgment-free companion for people who feel mentally overloaded and need help turning chaos into action.

## Who It Helps

- People with ADHD and executive-function overload
- Anyone stuck in task paralysis, time pressure, or emotional overwhelm
- Users who want practical support without shame

## Core Promise

- Speak or type what is on your mind
- Get empathetic understanding, not criticism
- Receive simple tips you can actually act on now
- Save what works so support gets more personal over time

## Core Experiences

- Brain dump capture (voice-first, with typing fallback)
- AI session summary with empathy + problem clarity
- Swipeable tips that feel doable in the moment
- Personal tip library built from what helped
- Session history to reflect on patterns and progress

## Product Tone

- Warm and emotionally safe
- Practical over perfection
- Non-judgmental and shame-free
- Focused on small wins and momentum
